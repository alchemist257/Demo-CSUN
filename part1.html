200sx & Me

August 19,2018

Ahh... the 1984 Nissan 200SX... Now this car has been a part of our family since before I was born!

The car where my mom learned to drive! The car that my brother and I learned to Drive! Im sure this car has been to a majority of the states on the westside!

What I think is the craziest thing is that it is still up and running! Not many people sti;; have their first car but we still do.

Granted it is very difficult to maintain because one, it wasnt such a famous car. Two not many car stores have thier parts!

So now in order to keep this cr alive we have to keep our eyes open to see if there are any that either people are selling or that are at our local junk yard.

The good thing is that with today's technology we are able to look on may websites like the facebook marketplace, LQk Pick UR Part (aka el pulpo), and last but not least craigslist.

Thanks to craigslist, I was able to find the car I have now today! The 1985 Nissan 200SX TURBO. The one that my father has is automatic and the one that I have is standard. His is grey and mine is blue.

When my father and I once went to a mechanic everybody was surprised to see two of the same car. As if one wasnt rare enough to see.

Im happy to be an owner of this car. I get to bond with my father more and we have something rare that most do not.

He just recently purchased another one too! This one is also automatic but instead it is from 1987.
